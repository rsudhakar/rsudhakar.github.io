The Tale Of Work Chair

Once upon a time, there lived an office chair so strong and balanced who is loved by all.

As she aged, The world was terrifed to see that its strength was only inside but not on the surface. But the master's love towards her was unchanging and decided to let the world know about it

He also wanted to show what she really is. Unique, Safe & Stable. So, She was reskinned to reflect her personality...

...with a pony tail

And she held happily ever after :)
